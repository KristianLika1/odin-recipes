Pasta Burro e Salvia

Description

This recipe is a classic Italian recipe, known for its simplicity but also for its deliciousness.

Ingredients

  • Pasta(any kind, long or short) 500g
  • Butter 150g
  • Parmigiano Reggiano or Grana Padano 100g
  • Sage 5 medium leaves
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. In a pot put some water to boil
  2. Meanwhile in a pan put on medium heat the butter, sage and black pepper
  3. Put the pasta in the water and cook it for 5 minutes less than the cooking time on the box
  4. Put the pasta in the pan and cook it with the sauce, adding cooking water and Parmigiano Reggiano to make a creamy sauce
  5. Serve in a plate and add some more Parmigiano and pepper to taste
  6. Enjoy!
