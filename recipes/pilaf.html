Pilaf

Description

This recipe is a dear recipe to me, it brings many happy memories.

Ingredients

  • Rice 300g
  • Olive oil
  • Broth(veg or non veg)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Spices(optional)

Steps

  1. In a pan put the rice and gently toast it onn medium heat until it turns golden brown
  2. Meanwhile in a pot make a broth either with some veggies* or with some meat*
  3. Add the broth just a an inch above the rice level
  4. Add the oil, salt, pepper and spices
  5. Keep adding the broth slowly while it gets absorbed
  6. Serve on a plate
  7. Enjoy!
