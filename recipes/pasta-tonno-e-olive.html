Pasta Tonno e Olive

Description

This recipe is a classic Italian recipe, known for its simplicity but also for its deliciousness.

Ingredients

  • Pasta(any kind, long or short) 500g
  • Tuna 240g
  • Olives 80g
  • Tomato sauce 700g
  • Garlic 2 cloves
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Oregano(optional)

Steps

  1. In a pan gently fry the garlic with some olive oil and pepper, meanwhile take a pot full of water and make it boil
  2. When the garlic turns golden, put the tomato sauce, the olives, half of the tuna salt and oregano
  3. Put the pasta in the pot, and boil it for 5 minutes less than the cooking time on the box
  4. Put the remaining tuna in the pan with the pasta and cook it with the sauce for 3 minutes(yes to make it al dente you must cook it less, otherwise it will be too soggy)
  5. Serve on the plate and put some pepper and olive oil to taste
  6. Enjoy!
